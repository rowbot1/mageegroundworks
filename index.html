Family-Run Excellence Since 1995

Established in 1995, Magee Groundworks LTD is a trusted family-run business with nearly three decades of experience in the groundwork industry. What started as a small family operation has grown into one of Northern Ireland's most respected groundwork specialists, serving Belfast and surrounding areas with the same dedication we've maintained since day one.

As a family business, we understand the importance of trust and reputation. Every project we undertake carries our family name, which is why we treat each job with the care and attention we'd give to our own property. From modest residential extensions to large-scale commercial developments, we bring the same dedication, expertise, and pride to every task.

Our success is built on strong relationships - with our clients, our suppliers, and our community. When you choose Magee Groundworks, you're not just hiring contractors; you're partnering with a family that takes genuine pride in laying the foundations for your dreams.
